Pricing

Basic Snap Show Magic Package – $600 for an event 4 hours or less. Includes 10 table cameras, a real-time slide show, a DVD package of all photos taken on Snap Show Magic cameras at the event, and all of the photos uploaded to and available on our website for a minimum of one year.

Basic Snap Show Magic Package Add-On – $100 for each additional hour of the basic Snap Show Magic package.

Basic Snap Show Magic Package Add-On – $10 for each extra camera beyond the first 10 included in the basic Snap Show Magic package.

Basic Snap Show Magic Package Add-On – $40 for photos delivered on USB flash drives. You will receive 2 flash drives with both containing all of the photos. Transferring the photos to USB flash drives takes less time than burning DVDs (about 15-30 minutes less) and they’re reusable (after you’ve backed up your photos).
